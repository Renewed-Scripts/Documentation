# Creating Oil Rigs

Admins can create new Oil Rigs on production, meaning you don't need to bother with config files. 

<iframe width="896" height="504" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/T981QFypeHo" title="YouTube video player" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture; web-share" allowfullscreen></iframe>


Simply use `/placerig` to place a new rig ingame.\
If an admin wants to transfer the rig simply just interact with it and click `Transfer Rig`.
